R.I.P.


Robert "Bobby" Allen Blackwell, Jr.

Father, Son, Uncle

11/17/1970 - 12/01/2024

A Great Father, Family Man, and Avid Cowboys Fan

In Loving memory of Robert Allen Blackwell Jr. of Livingston, Texas passed away peacefully on December 1, 2024, at the age of 54. Robert A. Blackwell was a man of simplicity and heart. Known for his laid-back nature and kind spirit, he found joy in the little things especially cheering on his beloved Dallas Cowboys.
Whether it was game day or just an ordinary afternoon, Robert passion for his team was unmatched. Robert cherished his family, friends, and the simple joys of life. Though he's no longer with us, his memory will live on in the hearts of those who had the privilege to know him. He will be deeply missed but forever remembered.
